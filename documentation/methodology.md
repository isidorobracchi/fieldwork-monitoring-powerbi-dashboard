\# Methodology



\## Data preparation



The dashboard was built by integrating multiple structured datasets related to survey monitoring and fieldwork progress.



The preparation phase included:



\- cleaning and standardizing survey identifiers;

\- harmonizing sampling strata codes;

\- checking consistency between sampling and interview data;

\- creating fields for filtering and aggregation;

\- preparing completion indicators;

\- defining progress classes for monitoring purposes.



\## Data modeling



The Power BI model connects sampling information, survey components, company identifiers and completed interviews through common keys.



The model was designed to support dynamic filtering across different levels of analysis, including:



\- survey component;

\- sampling strata;

\- company characteristics;

\- territorial information;

\- fieldwork status.



\## Indicators



The main indicators include:



\- completed interviews;

\- target interviews;

\- completion rate;

\- progress by strata;

\- progress by survey component;

\- coverage classes;

\- unmatched or inconsistent records.



\## Dashboard design



The dashboard was designed for operational monitoring, with a focus on clarity, fast interpretation and interactive exploration.



Visual elements include:



\- KPI cards;

\- tables and matrices;

\- progress indicators;

\- filters by survey component;

\- charts for completion classes;

\- views supporting consistency checks.



\## Privacy



The portfolio version uses anonymized screenshots and does not include raw data, personal data, company-level records or confidential datasets.
